Visit Fish Haven Idaho

If you want beach front, this is the place to be. Recreational activities at Bear Lake Adventure include beach and lake activities, participating in volleyball, horse shoes, croquette, basketball, frisbee throwing, etc. There's plenty of grass for playing games. Sleep in the cabin or bunk house (sleeps 16), or set up a tent on soft grass. There's parking for cars, trailers, and motorhomes. Bring your mountain bike, motorcycle, or 4-wheeler and ride up beautiful Fish Haven Canyon (1 block away). Within walking distance to stores for food, (raspberry shakes at Gladys!) deli, fuel, gifts, and the Idaho Lottery. Explore Minnetonka Cave in nearby Saint Charles Canyon. Visit the Oregon Trail visitors center and participate in a reenactment of the past. Take in a melodrama at Pickleville Playhouse in Garden City. Make use of our large covered bowery, which seats 80 at a time, for games and food.
